Children separated from mothers at India-Pakistan border
Families caught in bureaucratic nightmare; appeal to PM Modi for help
India asked all Pakistanis to leave after Pahalgam attacks
In the wake of the Pahalgam terror attacks, 11-year-old Zainab and 8-year-old Zenish face a tragic dilemma at the India-Pakistan border in Punjab, as both are being sent back to their country with their mother stuck in Delhi.

"Very difficult to leave my mother behind. My heart is broken," Zainab tells India Today TV, her voice breaking and her eyes welling up with tears.
